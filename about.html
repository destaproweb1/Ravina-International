About Ravina International

For over 15 years, we've been dedicated to providing professionals with workwear that combines durability, safety, and style.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

Building a Legacy of Quality

Founded in 2008 by industrial safety expert Talha, Ravina International began as a small workshop dedicated to solving the common problems faced by workers in demanding environments. Talha noticed that most workwear either sacrificed comfort for durability or style for functionality.

Our first breakthrough came with the development of our patented FlexWeave fabric technology, which combined breathability with exceptional tear resistance. This innovation set the standard for what would become our signature approach: creating workwear that doesn't force professionals to choose between safety and comfort.

Today, we serve over 50,000 professionals across construction, manufacturing, Safety, and outdoor industries, maintaining our commitment to quality while embracing sustainable manufacturing practices.
