Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 7-day refund policy for all items in case of damage item/change item. Custom orders and clearance items are final sale.

How long does shipping take?

+

we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days. Additional customs fees may apply.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days. Additional customs fees may apply.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

+

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, orders enter our processing system and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

Do you offer volume discounts for businesses?

+

Yes, we offer volume discounts for orders of 25+ items. Contact our wholesale department at info@ravinainternational.com for custom pricing and to discuss your specific needs.
